Definition List:

Apple
A fruit that grows on trees and is typically round, red or green, and crisp.
Banana
A long, curved fruit with a yellow or green skin and soft, sweet flesh.
Orange
A citrus fruit with a bright orange skin and juicy, segmented flesh.
